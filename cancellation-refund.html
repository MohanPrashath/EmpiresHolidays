Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellation Terms:
  • Cancellations made 30 days or more before the tour date will receive a 100% refund.
  • Cancellations made between 15-29 days before the tour date will receive a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made 14 days or less before the tour date are non-refundable.
  • No-shows on the day of the tour will not be eligible for any refund.
Refund Process:
  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your refund is initiated.
  • For any issues, contact our support team at empiresholidays@gmail.com.
Exceptions:

In case of natural disasters, political unrest, or pandemic-related travel restrictions, special cancellation terms may apply. Please reach out to us directly for assistance.
